FAQ Section

Do I need any previous experience?

No. Our activities are suitable for beginners, and our instructors will provide full guidance and safety briefings before you start.

What equipment is provided?

We provide all the equipment you need, including wetsuits, buoyancy aids, helmets, kayaks, paddleboards and paddles.

What should I bring?

Please bring swimwear, a towel, suitable footwear that can get wet, and a change of clothes for after your activity.

How old do participants need to be?

Minimum age requirements vary depending on the activity. Please contact us for details on specific sessions.

Are the activities safe?

Yes. Safety is our top priority. All sessions are led by experienced instructors, and weather, tide and sea conditions are carefully checked before every activity.

What happens if the weather is bad?

If conditions are unsafe, we may reschedule your booking or offer an alternative activity where possible.

Can families take part?

Absolutely. Paddleboarding and many kayaking sessions are perfect for families, beginners and those looking for a more relaxed coastal adventure.

Will we see wildlife?

Often! The Pembrokeshire coastline is home to seals, seabirds, dolphins and other marine wildlife, which you may spot during your activity.

How long do activities last?

Most sessions last between 2 and 3 hours, including safety briefings and equipment fitting.

How do I book?

You can contact us through our website, phone or email to check availability and make a booking.
